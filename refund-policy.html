Refund & Cancellation

Effective Date: August 14, 2024

Thank you for choosing RakhiAura, operated by RakhiAura Festive Traditions Private Limited. Since our products include custom handcrafted designs and gourmet sweets, we maintain a clear policy regarding refunds and cancellations.

1. Order Cancellations

You can request order cancellations within 12 hours of placing your order. Once an order is processed, packaged, or shipped, cancellations are no longer possible. To cancel your order, email us immediately at care@rakhistore.com.

2. Damage & Transit Issues

If your Rakhi box or gift hamper arrives damaged, defective, or with missing items, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery. To verify, please attach clean photos or an unboxing video in your email request. We will issue a free replacement or store credit.

3. Refunds Processing Time

Approved refunds are credited directly back to the original payment source (debit card, credit card, net banking, UPI). The bank settlement takes approximately 5 to 7 business days to reflect in your account.

4. Non-Refundable Items

Please note that personalized gift wrap boxes, custom greeting card print requests, and sweet hampers are non-returnable and non-refundable unless verified to be damaged during transport.
